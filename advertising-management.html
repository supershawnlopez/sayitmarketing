Common questions
What's the difference between Google Ads and social media ads?

Google Ads catch people who are actively searching for your service right now — high intent, ready to book. Facebook and Instagram ads reach people who match your ideal customer profile, even before they start searching. Together they cover every stage of how customers discover and choose you.

How much does it cost to advertise on Facebook or Google?

You set the budget — $100 to $500/mo is typical for most local businesses. That money goes directly to the platform under your account. Say It Marketing's flat monthly management fee covers setup, strategy, ad creative, optimization, and monthly reporting.

Do I need to sign a contract?

No contracts. Month to month. Your ad budget stays in your own account — you always own it. Cancel anytime, no penalties.

How fast will I see results?

Google Ads can start driving calls and clicks within 24–48 hours of launch. Social ads typically take 2–4 weeks to optimize and hit their stride. We monitor both daily and adjust as we learn what works in your market.

I'm already on a Say It Marketing plan — does that change my price?

Yes. Existing clients get a loyalty rate: $99/mo for one platform (instead of $125) or $149/mo for both (instead of $199). Just check the box when you submit your plan above.

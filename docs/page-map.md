# Page Map

## MVP Required Pages
1. `/` Home
Purpose: qualify quickly, present core offer, route to primary CTA.

2. `/website-packages`
Purpose: close one-time website sales (`$399/$599/$799`).

3. `/hosting-care-plans`
Purpose: convert recurring revenue.

4. `/social-media-services`
Purpose: secondary growth offer.

5. `/print-services`
Purpose: controlled upsell using wholesale-backed pricing.

6. `/get-quote`
Purpose: capture high-intent non-buyers.

7. `/free-website-audit`
Purpose: capture warm/cold leads.

8. `/contact`
Purpose: fallback capture path.

9. `/thank-you`
Purpose: confirmation + next-step routing.

10. `/results`
Purpose: proof and trust.

## SEO Expansion Pages (Phase 2)
- `/service-area/{city}`
- `/service/{city}/{service}`

## Home Page Content Rules
Keep on home:
- clear promise
- who this is for
- package starting prices
- short trust/proof
- primary CTA (`Start Your Website`)
- secondary CTA (`Get a Quote`)

Move off home:
- long technical details
- full feature grids
- deep FAQs/policies
- secondary operational content

# Print Offer SOP

## Positioning
Sell as retail service: "Done-for-you business printing."
Do not position trade suppliers publicly.

## Offer Placement
- Not core homepage path
- Use as upsell after website purchase
- Keep dedicated `/print-services` page

## Launch SKU Set (Phase 1)
1. Business cards
2. Flyers/postcards
3. Yard signs (coroplast)
4. Retractable banners
5. Simple storefront/window vinyl

## Pricing Rules
- Landed cost = print + shipping + payment fees + 3% risk buffer
- Margin floor = 35%
- Target margin = 45-55%
- Minimum gross profit per order = $40
- Custom/rush minimum gross profit = $75

## Payment + Approval Policy
- 100% prepay required
- written proof approval before production
- quote expires in 7 days
- rush fee applies on expedited jobs
- shipping passed through + handling fee

## Reprint Policy
- Reprint only for production defects
- No reprint for client-approved proof errors
- Defect claim window: define clearly in quote terms

## Operational Flow
1. Intake (specs, quantity, due date)
2. Quote with terms + expiry
3. Payment received
4. Proof generated and approved
5. Supplier order placed
6. Tracking sent
7. Delivery confirmation + upsell prompt

## What To Avoid Initially
- complex packaging/die-cut projects
- multi-SKU kitting
- apparel-heavy variant jobs
- high-risk event-deadline jobs without premium pricing

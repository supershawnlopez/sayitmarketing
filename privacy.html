Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Say It Marketing ("we," "us," "our") operates sayitmarketing.com. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What We Collect

When you submit a form on our site, we collect:

  • Your name, email address, and phone number
  • Your business name and the services you're interested in
  • Your approximate budget and contact preferences
  • Your consent to receive follow-up communication

We do not collect payment information directly. All payments are processed by Stripe. See Stripe's Privacy Policy.

How We Use It

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your inquiry and recommend the right service
  • Send you a confirmation email after your form submission
  • Follow up if you haven't heard from us within a few days
  • Improve our services based on what clients need

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for their marketing purposes. Ever.

Email Communication

Transactional emails (confirmations, follow-ups) are sent through Resend. By submitting a form and checking the consent box, you agree to receive these messages. You can opt out at any time by replying "stop" or emailing hello@sayitmarketing.com.

Data Storage

Your information is stored in a secure database managed by Supabase. We take reasonable precautions to protect your data but cannot guarantee absolute security.

Cookies

This site does not use tracking cookies or advertising pixels. Basic session behavior may use browser storage for functionality (like form state). No personal data is stored in cookies.

Your Rights

You can request to view, update, or delete your information at any time. Email us at hello@sayitmarketing.com and we'll respond within 5 business days.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the site after changes means you accept the updated policy.
